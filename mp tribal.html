MADHYA PRADESH TRIBAL MUSEUM

The Madhya Pradesh Tribal Museum in Bhopal takes you on an immersive journey through the culture of the local tribes of the state. With large colourful displays, the museum shows the art, traditions, daily life, and customs of a large number of indigenous groups.The Tribal Life Gallery has full-sized models of the tribesâ€™ houses and dwellings, large enough for visitors to go inside.
